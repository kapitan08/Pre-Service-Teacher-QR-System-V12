Help Center

• Dashboard – View attendance summaries and recent activity.
• Sections – Create and manage class sections.
• Students – Enroll, edit, or remove students and generate QR codes.
• QR Scanner – Scan student QR codes to record attendance.
• Gradebook – Enter per-item scores for Written Work (up to 7), Performance Task (up to 7), and Quarterly Exam (4 boxes). Averages are computed automatically using the DepEd formula.
• Class Schedule – Manage your weekly class timetable.
• Attendance Logs – View and export attendance records.
• Absent Tracker – Monitor present, late, and absent students.
• Print QR Cards – Generate printable student QR cards.

Privacy Policy

This portal stores student and attendance information locally in the browser for academic management purposes only.

• Student records are used solely for attendance tracking.
• Personal information is not shared with third parties.
• Data remains private and accessible only to authorized users.
• Users are responsible for protecting login credentials.
• Regular backups are recommended to prevent data loss.

Owner Insight


As the developer of this Student Attendance Management Platform, I created this system to integrate modern technology into education and provide a smarter, faster, and more reliable way of managing attendance.

This platform helps reduce manual work, minimize errors, and improve the accuracy of attendance records through features such as QR-based attendance scanning, student registration, automated reports, and real-time monitoring.

It is designed to support teachers and administrators in managing student data efficiently while promoting accountability, discipline, and transparency in the academic environment.

With a strong focus on usability, performance, and user experience, the system allows users to navigate easily while benefiting from practical and meaningful tools.

This project reflects a commitment to innovation in education and continuous improvement, with the goal of building smarter academic systems that support effective learning and management.

Thank you for supporting this project as it continues to grow and improve.
